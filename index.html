About me

I’m Subhash, a young Web Developer, currently located in Bangalore. After finishing my studies in 2014, I started my career as a front-end developer. I quickly learned that I loved design and after a year, in 2015, I joined a product based company as a web designer. During that time I mastered wireframing, user experience and user interface design. My skills, perseverance and desire to learn more have afforded me the opportunities to become a UI Designer and front-end developer.
